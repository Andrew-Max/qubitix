Developers and Dev Ops

{% include team_card_start.html name="Andrew Max" asset="andrew.jpeg" grid="col-lg-6 col-sm-12" %}

With 7+ years of experience developing software, Andrew has worked as everything from developer number two at a drone startup to a consultant for one of the five largest U.S. banks and the largest hospital system in Pennsylvania. He is deeply passionate about writing clean, well architected code, and prefers to work full stack.

Andrew spends his free time mountain biking, snowboarding, and traveling around the world and keeps a travel blog at andrew-max.github.io.

Andrew is the founder, CEO, and team lead at Qubitix.

Designers

{% include team_card_start.html name="Allison" asset="allison.jpg" grid="col-lg-6 col-sm-12" %}

Allison is a digital product designer with over 6+ years of experience designing for web and mobile. She spent 5 years working in Silicon Valley before heading out to travel the world and work remotely full time. Armed with her background in visual graphic design, her work combines a strong design aesthetic with data-driven, user focused UX thinking.

Allison spends her free time exploring via bicycle, seeking out live music, and petting office dogs.
